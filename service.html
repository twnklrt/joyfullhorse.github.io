Cross Connection Control Testing

STAT Climate Control Solutions are specialists for testing, repairing and installing cross connection control devices, also known as backflow valves. These devices are installed in commercial premises and newer residential sprinkler systems as a means to protect the municipal water supply from contamination (including; boiler chemicals, cleaners, pesticides, etc) by not allowing anything to enter the water supply in low flow conditions.
